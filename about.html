OUR STORY

Inye's Bakery was inspired by Benstowe Lisa Mieinye after a personal health
journey that deeply imapcted my outlook on food. Motivated by a desire to
make a positive change, in response to the health struggles of my father, who
is currently battling with diabetics, high blood sugar, and high blood pressure;
as a result, unhealthy diet habit, which included many processed sugar-laden
baked goods. seeing the lack of healthy, delicious baked options in Nigeria, I
was inspired to create a bakery that will focus on nutrious, low-sugar alternatives.
The aim is to bridge the gap in the market for healthier baked goods, offering
Nigerians tasty treats made with natural ingredients that suuport better health.

We hope that inye's Bakery will continue to be a Testament to my vision
and especially to those seeking better eating habits due to health concerns.

WHY YOU SHOULD BUY FROM US

You should buy from us because we offer high quality, freshly baked goods with superior taste and texture. Our bakery uses only natural ingredients
